About me

I am a seasoned software developer with over 8 years of experience in the industry. I currently hold the position of Technical Architect at Continental AG, where I specialize in designing and implementing scalable and efficient software solutions

Throughout my career, I have developed a robust skill set that includes expertise in Python, AWS Infrastructure using Cloud Development Kit (CDK), Django, Jenkins, Docker, and the basics of Machine Learning. My role at Continental AG involves leveraging these technologies to architect and develop complex systems that meet the needs of our clients and stakeholders.

One of my core strengths is my proficiency in AWS services, including EC2, Batch, Lambda, Step Functions, and CodePipeline. This, coupled with my experience in continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD) using Jenkins and Docker, enables me to deliver high-quality, reliable software solutions.

In addition to my technical skills, I am passionate about fostering collaboration and communication within my teams. I believe that the best software solutions are built when developers work closely with all stakeholders, including business leaders and end-users, to understand and address their needs.

As I continue to advance in my career, I remain dedicated to expanding my knowledge and expertise, particularly in emerging technologies and methodologies. I am always excited to take on new challenges and contribute to innovative projects that push the boundaries of what is possible in software development.
